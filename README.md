# 📊 Beauty E-Commerce Business Analysis

### 🔍 Business Problem

An e-commerce business lacks clear visibility into category-level performance and order trends, making it difficult to optimize product strategy and improve revenue generation.

---

### 🎯 Objective

* Analyze category-level revenue contribution
* Identify high-performing and underperforming segments
* Evaluate order trends and customer behavior
* Provide data-driven recommendations

---

### 📁 Dataset

* Brazilian e-commerce beauty dataset (public dataset)
* Includes order value, product category, and order status

Note: Dataset not included due to GitHub size limitations.

---

### 🛠 Tools Used

* SQL (Data extraction & transformation)
* Power BI (Dashboard & visualization)
* Excel

---

### 🔄 Approach

* Conducted **requirement analysis** to define KPIs
* Performed **data cleaning and transformation using SQL**
* Analyzed **category-level revenue distribution**
* Evaluated **order trends over time**
* Built an interactive dashboard for insights

---

### 📈 Key Insights

* Beauty category contributes ~74.91% of total revenue
* Revenue is highly concentrated in specific product segments
* Majority of orders are successfully delivered
* Demand shows fluctuations over time

---

### 💡 Recommendations

* Focus on scaling high-performing categories
* Improve performance of low-contributing segments
* Optimize product mix based on revenue trends

---

### 🧾 SQL Analysis

Performed data extraction, cleaning, and aggregation using SQL to support dashboard creation and business analysis.

---

### 🧠 Business Impact

Enabled data-driven insights into category performance and revenue trends, supporting strategic decision-making.
